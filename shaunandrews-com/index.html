Shaun Andrews

Shaun is a digital designer with over 20 years experience crafting and building interfaces and applications for millions around the world. Through his work at Automattic, he's helped create and improve numerous products and services for WordPress. Products like WordPress.com, WordPress.org, VaultPress, Jetpack, WooCommerce, and most recently Tumblr.

Shaun's style of working can be described as rapid, yet thorough. With a strong penchat for intuitive designs that reduce complex problems into simple systems. His direct, bold, and passionate style aims to rip through red-tape with pure honesty and collaboration. He's capable of working with established design systems, creating new systems, or working scrappy without any system at all.
